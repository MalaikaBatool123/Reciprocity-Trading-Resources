Flooring Materials

Floor covering refers to the material applied over a floor structure, providing a surface for walking. Although used interchangeably, 'floor covering' is more associated with materials that are laid without much fixation.

Carpets

A carpet, a textile floor covering, usually comprises an upper layer of pile attached to a backing. While traditionally wool was the primary material for the pile, nowadays polyester is frequently used due to its cost-effectiveness compared to wool.

Bed Sheets

Bed sheets are available in two primary styles: flat and fitted. A flat sheet is plain, while a fitted sheet is crafted with four corners specifically for use as the bottom sheet. Moreover, a fitted sheet can be fastened using a drawstring rather than elastic.
